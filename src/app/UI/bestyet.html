Frequently Asked Questions

What file formats do you support?

We support all major image formats including JPG, PNG, TIFF, and RAW files from most camera manufacturers. Our AI can process high-resolution images up to 100 megapixels.

Is my data safe?

Absolutely. We use bank-level encryption to protect your photos and personal information. Your privacy is our top priority. We also offer two-factor authentication for added security.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. There are no long-term commitments or cancellation fees. If you cancel, you'll still have access to your account until the end of your billing cycle.

Do you offer a mobile app?

Yes, we have mobile apps for both iOS and Android devices, allowing you to enhance your photos on the go. Our mobile apps offer most of the features available on our web platform.

How does AI photo enhancement work?

Our AI uses deep learning algorithms trained on millions of professional photos. It analyzes your image for various aspects like exposure, color balance, and composition, then applies targeted enhancements to improve these elements.

Can I undo AI enhancements?

Yes, all AI enhancements are non-destructive. You can easily revert to the original image or adjust the intensity of the enhancements at any time. We also keep a history of your edits for easy comparison.

What kind of customer support do you offer?

We offer 24/7 email support for all users. Pro and Enterprise plan subscribers also get priority support with faster response times and access to video chat support for complex issues.

Can I use the enhanced photos commercially?

Yes, you retain full rights to your enhanced photos and can use them for any purpose, including commercial use. Our AI enhancement is considered a tool, similar to traditional photo editing software.

How often do you update your AI models?

We continuously train and improve our AI models. Major updates are typically released quarterly, with minor improvements rolled out more frequently. All updates are automatically applied to ensure you always have access to the latest technology.

Is there a limit to how many photos I can enhance?

The number of enhancements depends on your plan. Basic users get 100 enhancements per month, Pro users get unlimited enhancements, and Enterprise plans are customized based on your needs.

Can I batch process multiple photos at once?

Yes, our platform supports batch processing. You can upload multiple photos and apply the same AI enhancements to all of them simultaneously, saving you time on large projects.

Do you offer any tutorials or learning resources?

Absolutely! We provide a comprehensive knowledge base, video tutorials, and regular webinars to help you get the most out of our AI photography tools. Pro and Enterprise users also get access to exclusive masterclasses.
